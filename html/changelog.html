TheDonkified updated:

  • Corner pouncing and corner throwing/grenade firing/etc have been added.
  • When thrown by another mob or propelled by another mob and you hit something dense (NOT A MOB), you will get damage. For example, you're fellow marines blows you up and you are thrown against a wall.
  • You can throw items over machines (such as vendors or consoles) as well as mobs. When trying to perform a high toss, there is a ~1 second timer before you perform the throw. It also has a maximum range of 4 tiles. If there are items that you think should not block high toss, feel free to make a suggestion in Gitlab.
  • To activate high toss, press CTRL+R or CTRL+click the throw HUD button. Note: if to untoggle throw, you need to follow the same procedure you used to toggle a throw type in the first place (e.g. you are in high toss throw mode, you need to press CTRL+R, CTRL+click the throw HUD button, or use your high toss macro to untoggle throwing). You can macro throwing, the commands are Toggle-Normal-Throw and Toggle-High-Toss.
  • You can walk through plastic flaps, there is a 2 second delay.
  • Smoke goes through girders now.
  • Flamethrowers deal damage to barricades (only when hitting them at the front) and resin structures.
  • Fire cannot go through the front of barricades. Acid spray cannot go through barricades at all.
  • Reworked how movement and slashing was handled with barricades and adjacent structures.
  • Diagonal movement exploits and bugs have been resolved.
  • You can slash people who are not fully protected by cover (behind an open plasteel barricade, not protected by both sides if you are attack from a corner, etc)
  • Throwing has been changed as well as how throw damage is calculated. Report any issues where something is too weak or too strong.
  • You can slash portable TComms again.

TobiNerd updated:

  • Fixed a bunch of symmetrical inconsistencies for lights on the Almayer.

Vampmare updated:

  • Added the S-V42 Prototype Smartpack. A backpack with inbuilt flashlight that unlocks special abilities if equipped by a Synthethic. First ability is Protective Form, the Synthetic gains insane protection but is unable to attack for a short duration. Second ability is Immobile Form, the Synthetic becomes unable to move, but also unable to be moved by others. Last ability is Repair Form, which just heals the Synthetic for a good bit damage after standing still for some time. All abilities have a cost, the backpack has a battery with charge and the only way to recharge the battery is by using GRAB intent on APCs, sucking out their power.
  • Added three colours of S-V42 Prototype Smartpack to Synthetic Vendors.
  • Human abilities, like the orders, now properly show when they're on CD and not.
  • IB now shows up properly on the Health Analyzer.
  • Embedded objects will now show up on the Health Analyzer.
  • Increased shrapnel text so its noticeable now.
  • Added kelotane, bicaridine and tramadol autoinjectors to WeylandMed Plus vendors, planetside ones.
  • Xenos can no longer drag DEAD and INFECTED bodies that won't have time to burst. So anyone infected just before they die become undraggable.
  • Dexalin Plus autoinjectors are now actually dexalin plus and not discount version.
  • Bleeding has been tweaked to be more sane.
  • Renamed the L42 to L42A battle rifle and changed its description.
  • Changed the gel fuel description to be more clear about what it does.
  • Increased nade falloff, effective range reduced as a result.
