  • Vanguard Praetorian Ability #1: Pierce. You use your tail to pierce enemies in a 1x3 line in front of you. If you hit at least 2 enemies, your shield is instantly recharged.
  • Vanguard Praetorian Ability #2: Dash. Charge through enemies to travel at most 4 tiles. You can reactivate the ability immediately after dashing to damage targets in a 3x3 grid around you. This also recharges your shield if it hits at least 2 targets.
